About Us

Building trust and excellence in construction across South Sudan since 2010.

Our Story

Promise Building Company Limited was founded in 2010 with a vision to contribute to the development of South Sudan through quality construction and reliable trading services. Based in Juba, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in the construction industry.

Our journey began with a small team of dedicated professionals and has expanded to include a workforce of over 100 skilled workers, engineers, and project managers. We have successfully completed over 200 projects across South Sudan, from residential buildings to commercial complexes and government facilities.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction and trading services that exceed client expectations, contribute to national development, and build lasting relationships based on trust, quality, and reliability.

Our Vision

To be the leading construction and general trading company in South Sudan, recognized for excellence, innovation, and our contribution to building sustainable communities.

Our Values

Integrity

Our Services

Comprehensive construction and trading solutions tailored to your needs.

Building Construction

We specialize in constructing residential and commercial buildings of all sizes. Our team of experienced engineers, architects, and construction workers ensures that every project is completed to the highest standards of quality and safety.

From initial planning to final completion, we manage every aspect of the construction process, including foundation work, structural framing, roofing, electrical and plumbing installations, and finishing works.

  • Residential houses and apartments
  • Commercial office buildings
  • Retail spaces and shopping complexes
  • Government and institutional buildings

Fencing

We provide secure and durable fencing solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial properties. Our fencing services include design, installation, and maintenance of various fencing types to meet your security and aesthetic needs.

Our team uses high-quality materials and modern techniques to ensure that your fence is not only functional but also enhances the appearance of your property.

  • Perimeter security fencing
  • Decorative garden fencing
  • Chain link fencing
  • Electric fencing systems
  • Gate installation and automation

Sale of Building Materials

As a leading supplier of construction materials in Juba, we offer a wide range of high-quality building materials at competitive prices. We source our materials from trusted suppliers to ensure durability and reliability.

Whether you're a contractor working on a large project or a homeowner undertaking renovations, we have the materials you need delivered to your site on time.

  • Concrete blocks and bricks
  • Sand, stones, and aggregate
  • Marram and soil for construction
  • Cement and other binding materials
  • Roofing sheets and materials
